Sport City
CHANG ARENA
CHANG ARENA

Welcome to the Thunder Castle.

Home to Buriram F.C., Thailand’s most trophied football team, the Chang Arena epitomises the city’s capacity to have fun and enjoy a sporting event.

Whenever the stadium floodlights are turned on the town adopts a serious party mode. Nothing comes close to the thunderous roar of the football fans as they fill up the 32,000 seater stadium, appropriately nicknamed the “Thunder Castle”.
