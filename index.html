True to our roots, the Grand Hotel Buriram emphasises both function and affordability - offering fellow wayfarers the best of local style and high-end amenities.

Opened originally in 1966 as the city’s first-ever hotel, the Grand Hotel Buriram has recently undergone a major refurbishment but still features mid-century retro design, such as our fabulous lobby and breakfast room, 1960s style furniture plus our original, restored hotel sign. Unique and centrally located, the 62-room Grand Hotel Buriram captures the vibrancy, soul and energy of Thailand’s famous “sport city” and invites you to stay and be inspired.

ROOMS

We welcome visitors from far and wide to enjoy your home away from home.

The Grand Hotel Buriram offers 62 rooms for travellers of all tastes and budgets. We have rooms in Standard, Deluxe, Grand Deluxe and Family Rooms, plus two Bunk Rooms fitted out with five bunk beds in each. Every chic interior has been individually designed for solo travellers, couples, family and friends to enjoy.

MotoGP Cancelled

Dear all guests,

Due to the recent official decision to cancel the Thailand MotoGP (scheduled to begin on 2-4 October 2020), the Grand Hotel Buriram will allow our guests who have made reservations and paid deposits to the hotel to:

1. Request to keep their deposit and guarantee reservation for the next MotoGP (contract signed until 2026).
or
2. Request for a full bank transfer refund (100% of the deposit rate) within 10 days.

To proceed, please fill out this form.

We will contact if we have any questions and refunds should arrive within 10 days.

Over the past six months we have participated in multiple community initiatives to fight COVID-19 including hosting Buriram Hospital’s doctors and staff during the peak of the pandemic. We have also provided free water to agencies and to those in need during the recent drought in the Buriram area.
