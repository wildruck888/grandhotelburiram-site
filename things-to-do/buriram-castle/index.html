BURIRAM CASTLE
BURIRAM CASTLE

A model of Phanom Rung temple, with an impressive new shopping centre, located between the football stadium and motor racing circuit.

This is a perfect blend of two periods in time. The beauty of the Khmer civilisation alongside a temple to modern lifestyle.

Apart from the Castle of Lightning, there is a cactus and rock garden, a community mall with restaurants, clothing stores, souvenir shops and motorsport apparel, It also features a kid zone, playground, spa, beauty centres, bank, ticket booths for the sports venues, a food court and nightlife venues.
