GROUP TRAVEL
The benefit of travelling with a group.
GROUP TRAVEL

In “Sport City” it’s all about who you roll with and the Grand Hotel invites you to bring your entire team and discover the best of Buriram and its sports venues and attractions.

We offer 62 chic rooms that are perfect for groups. If you are visiting the circuit or the football stadium, then look no further.

We know how much effort goes into organising a trip for your friends or fellow sports fans and a stay at the Grand Hotel will ensure that your group is well-rested, well-fed and treated with warmth and respect.

We also cater for groups exploring the Isaan region, family groups and student classmates sharing new experiences and making memories. Our spacious rooms are perfect for tour groups and as an intimate boutique hotel we know the value of family and invite you to call the Grand Hotel home for a little while. Our Family Rooms are stylish with great amenities, free Wi-Fi and a complimentary breakfast. Plus you can keep your whole clan together and even block off a section of rooms.

Our Bunk Rooms are ideal for students travelling together, with everything you need for a good night’s sleep and easy access to healthy meals and snacks – not to mention free Wi-Fi whenever you need it!

SPORTS WEEKENDS

This is the vacation you've been waiting for! Spend three days of excitement in Sport City, whether at a race weekend at Buriram International Circuit or football fun at the home of Buriram United.

Release your inner sportsperson in our newly revamped rooms at the Grand Hotel Buriram and take advantage of discounted tickets for your favourite spectator sport. Get prime perks like free daily breakfast, round trip airport transfers and free Wi-Fi. Enjoy top quality local cuisine, the famous night markets and parties all weekend!
