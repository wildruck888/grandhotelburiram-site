OUR JOURNEY

Our journey began in 1966 and we have come a long way since starting work on the original hotel. We’ve re-discovered many of the hotel’s Mid-century details and you’ll now find them as a telling part of the hotel’s redesign, setting a nostalgic but modern tone.

Check-In: 14:00
Check-Out: 12:00
Number of Rooms: 62
Types of Room: Five

Opened: 1966
Reburbished: 2020

The Grand Hotel in Buriram is rare – a contemporary hotel that has maintained its beautiful original features throughout, which embody the best of the 1960s.  The striking original building offers traditional charm, but brings an eclectic mix of the past and the present together with local artists and craftsmen, providing stunning artwork and expertise to create a truly delightful visual area and a unique experience.

With differently styled rooms throughout, we now offer a hospitable café area providing delicious continental breakfasts and light bites using locally sourced products. Besides the comfortable guest rooms, the hotel has the conveniences and services that one expects from a city hotel – laundry, elevator, shuttle service to and from Buriram airport and ample car parking facilities.

Only five minutes from the city centre and the railway station, the hotel’s strategic position ensures that guests can quickly reach all the local points of interest that Buriram has to offer. Should you have any special requests during your stay, a dedicated concierge team will be at your service whenever you need them. Trust us, you’ll love it!

FEEL GOOD HIT OF BURIRAM

Well-being and fun at the Grand Hotel Buriram is focused on a laid-back atmosphere which provides inspiration for you to become the most authentic version of yourself.

We care about our city, our environment, our future and welfare, making it our goal to be an eco-friendly hotel. We also encourage our guests to be eco-conscious, sort and recycle waste, minimise the use of plastic, save water and, of course, never forget to smile!
