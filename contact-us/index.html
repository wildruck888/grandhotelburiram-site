HOW TO GET HERE

Come witness the urban oasis that is the Grand Hotel Buriram, a centrally located boutique hotel featuring a perfect balance of retro-cool and casual sophistication.

Fun, family-friendly and comfortable, the Grand Hotel is the convenient choice and is within walking distance of the railway station and only 30 minutes transfer to Buriram airport, which the hotel can arrange. The bus station is also nearby and buses take around five hours from Bangkok (four hours by car).

   
A

Explore Buriram only a few steps from the hotel. The city is a friendly place and in recent years has become a key player in the Thai tourist market. As well as boasting one of the country’s top football teams and some of its best Muay Thai fighters, Buriram and the surrounding province offer a number of attractions for both local and foreign visitors alike.

  • Railway Station 250 metres
  • Buriram Airport 35 kms
  • Buriram Hospital 1000 metres
  • Bus Station 400 metres
  • Walking Street 500 metres
  • Buriram United 10 minutes
  • Buriram International Circuit 10 minutes
A

Getting to the Grand Hotel Buriram couldn’t be easier. Simply head into the centre of Buriram and look for the signs to the Town Centre.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us!
