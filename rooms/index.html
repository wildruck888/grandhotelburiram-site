OUR ROOMS
All you'd expect and much more!

Each of our 62 design-driven rooms gets you an uncompromisingly cushy bed, a walk-in shower you'll never want to get out of, free Wi-Fi plus a great selection of TV channels. A real home away from home.

A chic and cosy, modern and memorable, warm and welcoming haven for adventurous tourists, business travellers or sports fans, the Grand Hotel Buriram is easily accessible from the railway station, airport and all major roads. This is a new type of escape with all the character and charm of a boutique hideaway, served up with heartfelt personal service and an easy-going at-home atmosphere. This is what we call travel for all!

PARADISE FOUND

Buriram is the provincial capital of its namesake province in the Northeast of Thailand. As well as its famous its sports venues, it makes a fabulous base for exploring some of the archaeological sites that are scattered around the province.

In the last couple of decades, hotel lobbies have become the centrepiece of the guest experience. In a matter of seconds, the guests soak up the atmosphere and make up their minds whether they like the hotel or not. For many guests, socialising in the lobby is one of the most memorable experiences they take from their trip. This is the place where new friendships are made, stories and jokes are told, and experiences are shared. We therefore hope that our Lobby is a suitable area for guests to interact freely with other guests and also our local regulars.
